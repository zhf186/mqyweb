# Product Overview

## 漫骑游 (Manqiyou) - Premium E-Bike Tourism Platform

A high-end cross-border cycling tourism platform integrating German-engineered E-BIKEs with curated deep-exploration cycling routes.

### Core Mission
"骑遇无限美好人生" (Encounter Infinite Beautiful Life Through Cycling)

### Product Positioning
- **Target Market**: Premium cycling tourism combining E-BIKE rentals with immersive local experiences
- **Key Differentiators**: 
  - German-engineered electric-assist bicycles (途尔 E-BIKE)
  - Deep-exploration routes integrating local culture, history, and nature
  - Comprehensive ecosystem: routes, local goods, community, membership
  - Online (web/app) + offline (lifestyle stores) integration

### Business Model
1. **Product Serviceization**: Deep cycling routes as core product with integrated local experiences
2. **Service Platformization**: Six functional divisions supporting franchise expansion
3. **Revenue Diversification**: Route fees, E-BIKE rental/sales, merchant commissions, memberships, events

### Brand Aesthetic
**"Futuristic Luxury Cycling"** - Fusion of:
- **Tech/Future**: Smart E-BIKE systems, innovation
- **Nature/Organic**: Outdoor, eco-friendly, sustainable
- **Refined/Luxury**: Premium quality, high-end service

### Key Features
- E-BIKE rental and sales
- Curated cycling routes with difficulty levels and categories
- Local goods marketplace
- Membership tiers with benefits
- Community events and social features
- Partner/franchise ecosystem
